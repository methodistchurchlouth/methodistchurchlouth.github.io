Open House

Every Wednesday from 10am - 1pm .

Wednesday is a Market day in Louth and with Louth Methodist Church being a stone's throw from the Market place this is the ideal place to come to meet up. We have hot drinks or a sandwich lunch and of course delicious cakes produced by the members of Louth Church.
The prices are kept affordable and the warm welcome is guaranteed.
