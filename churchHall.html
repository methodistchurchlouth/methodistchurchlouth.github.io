Church Hall

Description

Occupancy: 375.

Size: 17m x 19.4m

The main church hall is a lovely space which was refurbished in 2015, and comes with built in audio visual systems, including projector and screen (own laptop needed), speaker system and induction loop.
This space is regularly used for concerts and talks using the front platform as a stage. The rear of the chuch is used for open house coffee mornings.
