Louth Methodist Church community groups

Louth Methodist Church hosts and runs various community groups throughout the week.

Internal Groups

Exalt:Family-friendly worship at Louth Methodist Church every Sunday from 9.30am -10.15am followed by refreshments. A mix of songs, stories, puppets, craft, and puzzles in a warm and friendly atmosphere.
Ladies' Group:Fortnightly meetings on Wednesdays at 7 - 9 pm in Hall 2.
Open House:Wednesdays 10am - 1pm in main church, incorporating Wednesday Worship every 1st Wednesday of the month at 12 noon for 30 mins approximately. A warm welcome to all.
Women's Fellowship:Tuesday afternoons from 2-3.30pm in Hall 2.
3-2-1:Meet with 3 things to do (songs, craft and refreshments), 2nd Friday of each month, 1 time a month. Drinks available from 9.00am. Stories and songs start at 9.30am. £2 donation to attend which includes refreshments and crafts.

External Groups Meeting Regularly

Darby & Joan:Weekly meetings between 1.45 and 3.30pm on Monday afternoons. A warm welcome to all over 60s!
Louth Wind Orchestra:Weekly meetings 6.30-8pm on a Thursday.
Louth & Keddington Women's Institute:Meet on a Thursday afternoon (monthly) for Main Meetings.
Louth People's Orchestra (LPO):Rehearsals on a Sunday afternoon twice monthly as well as concert/ coffee mornings.
Louth Riverhead Silver Band:Meet for band practice on Thursdays (fortnightly) at 7pm.
Louth Textile Group:Meet Friday evening each month as well as day sessions on particular skills/techniques/crafts.
Louth Theatre Group:Rehearsals take place on a regular basis.
TableTennis4All:Table tenis group which meets every Thursday evening at 7.30pm.
U3A:Main meeting Tuesday afternoon monthly. In addition specialist groups within U3A meet for Calligraphy, German and other study days.

Uniformed Organisations

Beavers:Meet on Tuesday evenings (term times).
Brownies:Meet on Wednesday evenings and Thursday evenings (term times); 2 groups.
Cubs:Meet on Tuesday evenings (term times).
Guides:Meet on Monday evenings (term times).
Rainbows:Meet on Wednesday and Thursday evenings (term times); 2 groups.
Scouts:Meet on Monday evenings (term times).

External Groups

Children's Links:Use a variety of rooms for courses/advice and job club drop ins on a regular basis.
Kettle's Funeral Directors:Hold an annual Remembrance Service in main church.
Lincolnshire Wildlife Trust:Meet at Louth MC in the main church hall for meetings and presentations 6 times a year.
Louth Male Voice Choir:Hold their annual Young Musician of the Year competition in main church.
Meridian Singers:Hold an annual concert in the main hall.
St Barnabas Hospice:Hold their annual Light up a Life Service in the main church hall.
