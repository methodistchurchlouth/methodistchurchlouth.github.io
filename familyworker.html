Church Family Worker

Claire Plowright

Claire Plowright is our part time Church Family Worker,
based at Louth Methodist Church in the East Lincolnshire Methodist Circuit..

Her role is to support church events in order to build relationships. For anyone who is a fringe members or occasional visitor to the church, Claire will be able to help signpost you to relevant activities and groups in order to help give you a feeling of belonging. Claire's main aim is to help everyone go from just attending church to belonging and contributing.

Claire is usually around at Sunday's Exalt service, and will soon have a new contact number which we will share here.
