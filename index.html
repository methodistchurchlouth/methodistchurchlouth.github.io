Louth Methodist Church

Welcome to Louth Methodist Church, find us here.

An enormous set of buildings which have stood there in various forms since the early 19th century, underwent an extensive refurbishment in 2015 in order to become a true Community Centre providing a host of activities for all ages. The groups are listed on separate pages.

Worship is of course at the heart of all we do. The worship area has been modernised to provide a comfortable multi-purpose hall. Come and see for yourself during our church services or Open House on a Wednesday morning.

Worship

Exalt, for the young and young at heart, is a modern form of worship and commences at 9.30am followed by refreshments.

The main traditional service is preceded by refreshments and commences at 10.45am. Singing the Faith is used as our Hymn book. As members of Churches Together in Louth we do have joint services on occasions and the newsletter listing all services is always available on this site.

The evening service commences at 6pm and is usually held in Hall 2, which is a more suitable environment for the smaller gathering.

Wednesday worship is held every first Wednesday of the month in the main church during Open House, starting at noon and lasting approximately 30 minutes.
